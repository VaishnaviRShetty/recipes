Hot Chocolate

Description

This is a very strong and rich recipe. Great for chocolate lovers!

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups milk
  • ½ teaspoon brown sugar
  • ½ teaspoon maple syrup
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon honey
  • 3 ½ tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ cups milk

Steps

  1. Heat 2 1/2 cups milk in a saucepan over medium heat until steaming, about 5 minutes.
  2. Stir in brown sugar, maple syrup, honey, and vanilla extract. Bring to boil and stir in cocoa powder.
  3. Remove from heat and add the remaining 1/2 cup milk.
  4. Return saucepan over heat and simmer until heated through, about 2 minutes more.
  5. Ladle into cups to serve.
